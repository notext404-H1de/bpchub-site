**Effective date: July 19, 2026**

This policy describes how BPC Hub handles information when you visit `blog.bpchub.com`. The site is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

## Information submitted directly to the blog

The initial blog does not provide a contact form, account system, comments, newsletter signup, or published blog email address. It therefore does not ask you to submit personal information directly. Before any of those features are enabled, this policy and any required consent controls will be updated.

## Site operation and hosting

The initial version of BPC Hub does not install an advertising pixel, analytics service, account system, or newsletter cookie. The site is hosted using GitHub Pages. GitHub and network providers may process technical request information needed to deliver, secure, and operate the service, such as IP address, browser data, request time, and requested page. Their handling is governed by their own policies.

If analytics, advertising, comments, accounts, a contact form, or a newsletter are added later, this policy and any required consent controls will be updated before those tools are enabled.

## Affiliate and external links

BPC Hub may link to Amazon and other external sites. Following an external link takes you to a service with its own cookies, identifiers, terms, and privacy practices. A retailer may record that a visit came from an affiliate link so it can attribute an eligible purchase. We do not control those external services.

## How information is used and shared

Technical information processed by hosting and network providers may be used to deliver the site, detect abuse, meet legal obligations, and protect rights and security. BPC Hub does not sell personal information. External providers handle information under their own policies.

## Retention and choices

Browser controls can limit or delete cookies used by external sites. Requests concerning data controlled by an external retailer or hosting provider should be directed to that provider.

## Future contact channel

The dedicated BPC Hub domain mailbox is not active yet, so no blog contact address is currently published. A working contact method will be added here before the blog begins collecting reader-submitted information.

We may update this policy as the site changes. A material revision will receive a new effective date on this page.
