BPC Hub stands for **Beauty & Personal Care Hub**. We publish practical English-language guides for readers in North America who want to understand personal care products without turning every purchase into a research project.

Our goal is simple: make the decision clearer. That means identifying who a product or category may suit, explaining the criteria that matter, and naming the tradeoffs that marketing copy often skips.

## What we cover

- Skincare routines and product formats
- Hair care products and tools
- Everyday body care and grooming
- Buying frameworks, label literacy, and product comparisons

## What independence means here

BPC Hub is independently operated. Editorial priorities are not sold to brands. If we use affiliate links, they are labeled and may generate a commission, but the commercial relationship does not change the price you pay or guarantee a positive recommendation.

We are building this publication deliberately. New guides are reviewed before publication, material corrections are noted, and pages are updated when product details or reliable guidance changes.

Read our [Editorial Policy](/editorial-policy/) for the complete process behind our guides and recommendations.
